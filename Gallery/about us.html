allu arjun


Allu Arjun was born on 8 April 1983[15][16] in Madras (present-day Chennai) to film producer Allu Aravind and Nirmala.
[17][18] His paternal grandfather was the film comedian Allu Ramalingaiah, while his paternal aunt is married to Chiranjeevi.
On 6 March 2011, He married Sneha Reddy in Hyderabad.[19] They have a son named Allu Ayaan and a daughter named Allu Arha.[20]
In 2016, Allu started a nightclub named 800 Jubilee in collaboration with M Kitchens and Buffalo Wild Wings.[21]
